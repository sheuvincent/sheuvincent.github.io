Vincent Sheu


Law/Computer Science student at Stanford University, but a Cal Bear at heart. Interested in issues at the intersection of law/policy and science/technology: cybersecurity, privacy, entrepreneurship/venture capital, intellectual property, and how all of the above can come together to make the world a better place. I've spent summers/quarters with the National Security Agency, U.S. Attorney's Office, and Perkins Coie LLP, and will be at Davis Polk & Wardwell LLP this summer.

In my spare time, I enjoy eating my way through cities, cheering on Bay Area sports teams, and getting to know people's life stories. Occasionally, I'll teach Rubik's Cube lessons, accidentally end up on SportsCenter/Above the Law (sometimes at the same time), or spend an evening at the symphony.
