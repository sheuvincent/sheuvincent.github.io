Vincent Sheu


Law/Computer Science student at Stanford University, but a Cal Bear at heart. I've summered with the National Security Agency and Perkins Coie LLP, and will be at Davis Polk & Wardwell LLP this summer. At Stanford, I am a part of the Stanford Law Review, and I was Editor-in-Chief of the Stanford Technology Law Review. I've also led or co-led the Asian Pacific Islander Law Students Association, Stanford Entertainment & Sports Law Association, Stanford Law and Technology Association and Stanford National Security and the Law Society. I'm interested in issues at the intersection of law/policy and science/technology: cybersecurity, privacy, entrepreneurship/venture capital, intellectual property, and how all of the above can come together to make the world a better place.
In my spare time, I enjoy eating my way through cities, cheering on Bay Area sports teams, and getting to know people's life stories. Occasionally, I'll teach Rubik's Cube lessons, accidentally end up on Sportscenter/Above the Law (at the same time), or spend an evening at the symphony.
